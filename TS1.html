Dinner

Steak and Hasselback Potatoes

A plated dinner that feels elevated without asking for restaurant-level technique.

1 serving Oven + skillet Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 1 potato
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • 1 steak
  • Black pepper
  • Montreal steak seasoning

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Wash and dry the potato, then place it between two chopsticks or butter knives to protect the base while slicing.
  3. Cut the potato crosswise into thin slices without cutting all the way through.
  4. Melt the butter, then combine it with olive oil and salt.
  5. Brush the butter mixture over the potato, making sure it gets between the slices.
  6. Bake for 1 hour and 20 minutes, brushing with more butter mixture every 20 minutes.
  7. During the last 20 minutes, heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
  8. Season the steak on both sides with salt, pepper, and Montreal steak seasoning.
  9. Sear the steak until it reaches your preferred doneness, then rest it for 5 minutes.
  10. Plate the steak with the potato and serve immediately.
