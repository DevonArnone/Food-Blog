Skillet Sandwich

Philly Cheesesteak

A savory sandwich layered with seasoned sirloin, peppers, onions, and melted Swiss cheese.

4 servings 40 minutes Weeknight

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 pound sliced sirloin
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 3 ounces Swiss cheese
  • 4 hoagie rolls

Directions

  1. Mix the dry seasonings together in a small bowl.
  2. Slice the onion and green pepper.
  3. Toss the steak with the seasoning blend and olive oil.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and cook the steak until browned.
  5. Remove the steak, then saute the onion and pepper until tender and caramelized.
  6. Preheat the oven broiler.
  7. Fill each hoagie roll with steak, peppers, and onions.
  8. Top with Swiss cheese and place on a sheet pan.
  9. Broil until the cheese has melted, then serve hot.
