American Cheeseburger

A classic backyard-style burger built around simple seasoning and a reliable sear.

4 servings 30 minutes Beginner

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds 80/20 ground beef
  • Salt, pepper, or burger seasoning blend
  • 4 slices cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, and onion
  • Condiments of choice

Directions

  1. Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the beef into four equal portions and shape into patties.
  3. Create a small indentation in the center of each patty to help it cook evenly.
  4. Season both sides with burger seasoning or salt and pepper.
  5. Cook for 3 to 5 minutes per side, depending on thickness and preferred doneness.
  6. Add cheese during the last minute so it melts over the patties.
  7. Toast the buns if you want more texture and structure.
  8. Build the burgers with condiments, vegetables, and the hot patties.
  9. Serve immediately with your preferred side.
