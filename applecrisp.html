Apple Crisp

Warm apples, cinnamon, oats, and a crisp topping that holds its texture beautifully.

16 servings 60 minutes Crowd Pleaser

Ingredients

  • 10 to 11 cups sliced tart apples
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon, divided
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup cold butter
  • Vanilla ice cream, optional

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Toss the apples with sugar and half of the cinnamon.
  3. Transfer the apples to a greased 13x9-inch baking dish.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine flour, brown sugar, oats, baking powder, nutmeg, and the remaining cinnamon.
  5. Cut in the butter until the topping resembles coarse crumbs.
  6. Sprinkle the topping evenly over the apples.
  7. Bake for 50 to 60 minutes, until the apples are tender and the top is deeply golden.
  8. Serve warm, ideally with vanilla ice cream.
